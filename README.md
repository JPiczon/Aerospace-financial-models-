Building a body of knowledge on Aerospace & Defense businesses 

Financial models and investment write-ups on aerospace and defense companies I find interesting — built as independent research.

The work follows a capital cycle lens: I'm most interested in where competitive position, return on invested capital, and free cash flow generation follow a legible physical and industrial logic — supply chain bottlenecks, sole-source dynamics, aftermarket pricing power, and the capacity cycles that drive (or destroy) returns.
